To calculate the grade needed on the last assignment for a specific grade:

  1. Enter '-1' in the numerator of the final assignment
  2. Enter your desired numerical grade in the 'result' input field
  3. Complete the fields as normal

*This feature only works with the final assignment. The result of this calculation will replace the numerator of the assignment.
