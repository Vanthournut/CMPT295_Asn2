Welcome to the Tokidex!

This site will help you manage, view, and register the Tokimon of world!

To view a specific Tokimon, proceed to the 'Display Tokimon' page. You will need to enter the Tokimon id to find the Tokimon. From there you can view the full information of that Tokimon, and delete it from the Tokidex if you choose.

To view the entire Tokidex database, proceed to the 'Complete Tokidex' page. You will be able to view all the Tokimon currently registered in the Tokidex, and you will be able to sort them based on your prefered attribute.

To register a new Tokimon, proceed to the 'Register Tokimon' page. Fill in the form on that page to add your new Tokimon to the Tokidex!
